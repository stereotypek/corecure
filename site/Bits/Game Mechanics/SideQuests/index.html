Cecelia and Diana

two twin girls, one pink and one purple. They follow around Myazaki to an obsessive extent. They’re like Mya’s little sisters, and take everything Mya says as law. Will defend Mya til the day they die. Diana is optimistic and trigger happy. Despite being a nurse, she carries a pistol with her at all times. She smiles in even the strangest situations, and finds herself giggling at almost everything. She likes to joke around with her patients and say, “uh oh! This parts infected! We’ll cut it out~ Diana, please give me the chainsaw!” Cecelia is the more level-headed one. The compassionate, though quieter girl of the twins. She still remains optimistic but is a lot gentler with what she does. She caries around a sniper rifle with her, also despite being a nurse. She’s incredibly obsessive over Mya even to the point of deterring Diana from approaching Mya too much. Cecelia frankly doesn’t care, and is just happy to have something to do. Myazaki a brute-force sort of girl. Similarly to Honeycakes, she was a war veteran, but turned into a nurse. Or, she’s trying to. She’s very strict and stern, like an old traditional woman. She has a harsh glare and generally scares everyone away when she approaches them. Looks like an absolute punk. Incredibly muscular, but does her best to be gentle. However, she usually ends up destroying it anyway. Mya was initially weary of Cecelia and Diana, but since they were both nurses, she decided to keep them around to hopefully ease everyone’s worries about her.
