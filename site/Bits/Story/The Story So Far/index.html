The human race has gone extinct.
From greed came war, and from war came disease.
Animals began to mutate into grotesque creatures, which would die from their own overgrowth. Plants would develop their own sentience, teeth and organs. The disease would eat the very cells in the human body.
The only things left were the Rumors which crawled the Earth. And from the ashes, they grew into a thriving species of vaguely humanoid figures.
They’d built a society over time, shockingly similar to the human races. From artifacts, they deciphered languages. From abandoned buildings came flourishing cities. Art was re-learned, computers were re-invented, and wars were re-fought.
In 1981, Beau would come into rule, bringing with him a new innovative age of technology, automation, and a capitalistic hell. Historians call it the Beginning of the End.
The Beau line would slowly devolve into capitalistic tyranny, all the way until 2012, when God Himself fell from the heavens.
The rapture had begun.
Angels crashed and burned, volcanoes erupted and the Earth trembled in it’s wake, bringing with it a cold and bitter wind. Historians estimate that 75% of the world was entirely wiped.
As the dust clears, a pulsating, white CORE rest in a small mount of cotton.
The PRODIGAL CORE was rumored to be the Core of Life, the Heart of God.
It’s the ultimate mother’s womb, it has the ability to breathe the breath of life into any creature given to it. Gamma was born into a 16-year-old-female-titanium skeleton within generation 3 ( even though Honeycakes remains to be ~10 years older than her), and is the second youngest clone in that generation outside of Epsilon. She was born prematurely, but is still quite healthy.
She never particularly stood out when fighting for Pyrrhon, except until when she’d left FUTURE. Everyone else kind of ignored the newer g3 clones, and many (like Phi, Chi, and Muse) didn’t even know that they existed.
Because of her general.. being-ignored-thing, she was paired up with another Low-level clone— a cranky old gen-2, half-working piece of scrap metal named Characters/Bosses/Prodigal|Prodigal. Over Gamma’s lifetime (from years 8-17) she grew extremely close to Characters/Bosses/Prodigal|Prodigal, almost to the point of an obsession. Prodigal could have quite honestly cared less. Anyway, one day, Prodigal came up with a full map of FUTURE which had been dropped off of an abandoned hard-drive while she was dumpster diving.
They began hatching a plan, a plan no one had thought of before: to get out of FUTURE once and for all. From 6 months of planning came the great escape from FUTURE. Their life, right in their hands, so close—

Constantine was born into a little family in current-day Philipines, up in Bontoc. He lived in a small village, with very little access to technology except for things like fliphones and creaky computers. This is because it rains almost all the time, and most of the houses are made of tar, planks, leaves, metal sheets, etc.
His parents never really let Constantine out of the house. It was against their religion to abort or abandon him, so they just locked him inside of the closet so he wouldn’t rat them out. They got tax benefits, and played it off as, ‘he’s very sick’. He was raised entirely online and by the internet, through chat rooms and such. He spent most of his time on (YouTube), scrolling on social media, involving himself in cheesy old forums, playing old games, etc.
On one particularly bad night, Constantine was almost fatally injured by his father experimenting to make illegal drugs at home. He had walked in at bad times and ended up with awful chemical burns which left his face extremely disfigured and ugly. To cover them up, he wears a welding mask (he does actually know how to weld, since it’s what he does for a living.)
As he got older, he saw a few videos of people doing live surgeries on clones and other machines, and developed such a large hyperfixation for it, he’d actually managed to write and create a master guide for clone, CORE, and Pi-Bot mechanics, due to his extensive research and abundance of time. This was a revolutionary thing, since it’d never been compiled so easily for everyone.
He also got a lot better at coding from this, and eventually got a few jobs (he’d illegally gotten into a few databases for his master list).
As Constantine grew older and more isolated, he built up his online reputation more and more. He works as a mechanic, but also as a blackmailer. his work partner is a girl named XETA, who has a huge crush on him but he’s gay. It’s rare since they’re usually destroyed on impact by default. He sold the data to some people on the internet, then got quite a bit of money.
