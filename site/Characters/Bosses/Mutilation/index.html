Backstory

Made alongside Prodigal. When Pyrrhon created her, she mutated. Pyrrhon was disgusted, and muttered, “She’s mutilated.” That became her name.

Mutilation has an obsession with Pyrrhon as her motherly figure. Pyrrhon keeps her away, but Mutation knows almost all of her secrets because she’s so nosy (including all of her clones and dirty secrets), so Pyrrhon is forced to spoil her. She’s gained immortality and doesn’t age. She steals body parts from soldiers she likes and places them inside of hers. Obviously some parts are already misplaced (the eye, mouth, etc were in that place at conception) so she has to put them there. She has Khan’s leg and Chou’s eye as of right now. She attempted to take Honeycakes (or as she knew him, OMEGA)’s leg when Khan’s got old and rotted, but they struck up a deal instead and OMEGA provided her with lots of other people for her to take instead of his. mutilation has an obsession with being a mother, which was by proxy of Pyrrhon when Nea was conceived.
