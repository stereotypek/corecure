a brief introduction on how to read the location system in CORE.

Now, a typical business card might look something like this: “Great    Food! Nice  Snacks!  Iron  Lantern   Street Grlil Reel , autentic   meat  at 50% price!! Come check on  out  us. Sampaguita, Phisit; HBNH - DN3807.” … with better English, most of the time.

The code at the bottom describes the exact location.

From largest to smallest, it goes from Hà-Binh, a country Phisit, a county Sampaguita, a city DN(code), direct address within that city.

  • DN stands for District Number. The code works like this:
    • First three letters are for the exact street. in this case, street 38.
    • The other two are for where the building is exactly located. 07 means that it is the seventh building from the right side.
