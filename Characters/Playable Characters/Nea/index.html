daughter of Marian

Personality

Nea, overall, is an egotistical brat. Yes, she’s smart enough to back that up, but she thinks awfully highly of herself. She’s horribly selfish, she only works for herself, and herself only. No one needs to know more about her. With that, she’s stoic and cold and would barely respond to you. She’s got a slender and tall build, with a hefty ponytail which could blanket a small child. Nea’s a feisty little thing. She’s aggressive and passionate, but tries not to let it slip out to preserve her image. She can be extremely rude, but sometimes she isn’t aware of it, so you’ll just have to tell her directly. She tries not to show any emotion, but she isn’t great at it. She comes across as more of a “Tsundere” than a cold and serious person once you get to know her. She’s pretty intimidating and likes to stay organized and on-top of things. She’s a perfectionist to a fault. _Psychotically smart, Nea’s got some pretty fucked up ideas and thoughts in her head.

Backstory

Nea’s origins are unknown to her. She woke up in a temple and grew up in a temple. One of the first students of General Worldbuilding, she grew as an exceptional musician and an expert at manipulating magic to her will. Age 18 was the day to take over her father. She doesn’t really like her father. Cold and stoic and harsh, that’s all. No loving relationship whatsoever, barely even a father. And God knows what sort of mother she might’ve had should she had lived. At age 18, a woman in violet and black robes waltzes into the lobby. Recognized as Pyrrhon, world leader. War leader. The only person in power and the only person to remain in power. Nea could really care less. Until she walks into her room and sees her father’s corpse, mangled and burnt. And who else to stand above it but that woman? “My daughter,” she says to Nea. “I found you.” ”I am not your daughter.” Her father coughs up blood. Marian, renowned warrior, accomplished magician. Cowering at her feet like an animal. Marian reaches into the depths of his chest, and out comes a beating heart. Not a heart, a CORE. A machine of dreams and delusions. Nea looks up at Pyrrhon. Pyrrhon looks down at her daughter. Nea’s a skilled swordsman. Launching forward in a flurry of attacks, but not enough! Pyrrhon’s quick on her feet, Pyrrhon’s smart. Why else is she a world leader? Her father lays motionless. Pyrrhon only grins. All Nea remembers from that day is running out of the temple, now in flames. Centuries of history and dreams of the unknown, all dancing in the wind with the flames. She looks down at her fingers, burning with flames. She looks up at the temple again, and feels nothing. Five years later, no one knows it was her. Not that many people knew that temples still existed, anyway. Much less that one burned down in the middle of nowhere. She attends schools and colleges, since what else could you do? But slave away her magic for a machine which rejected the very idea of her? Nea only bites her teeth and steels herself. No point in fighting a losing battle. That is, until a little clone named Gamma stole something particularly important to her.

Playstyle

Role: Classic all rounder, self-sustainer

While she has a pretty heavy lean into magic attacks, she’s quite slow compared to others. She’s a mid-ranged attacker who can dish out all kinds of attacks and heal herself. She’s more of a jack of all trades (with a heavy lean into her special attacks).

Signature Ability: RHYTHMIC DISSONANCE

Each combo hit above 5 will add an increased +1% damage to the next hit. this stacks on top of other bonuses ie 6 hit: 101% dmg 7 hits: 102% dmg (+2% of it's normal damage) 8 hits: 103% dmg ... etc. This value resets once the combo ends

Extended Ability: Counterpoint!

If Nea is hit mid-swing by a PHYSICAL attack, then trigger a parry slash. Also, gain the ability to cancel an enemy attack once every 5 seconds.

Base: Swords Dance

Exactly what it sounds like! Nea does two quick slashes then a down swing.

Shift: Grace Notes

All melee attacks gain +1 phantom hit at 50% damage (visually distinct, hits slightly after the main strike) These phantom hits count toward combo count and RHYTHMIC DISSONANCE bonus. All phantom hits deal magic damage. Parrying during Grace Notes automatically resets the cooldown of your next combo finisher, letting you loop offense faster. HOWEVER Nea will eventually get bored and tired of this, so SHIFT can only last for about 12 seconds before she goes back. 20 second cooldown between each.
