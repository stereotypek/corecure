Backstory

Tto talk about Shenx we need to talk about Marian

Nea was born, and Marian went on a mission (his last mission). He took her, Khan, and Chou (his friend) with him to finish up the mission. The mission was to basically put down a rebellion/rebel group (it was TLH...)

Clementine

Shenx. His parents were DEEPLY involved in TLH, and so was he. He was a fierce warrior even at like. Age ten. So of course he fought with them!

Marian was kind of giving up (this is the night he left with Nea) and Khan was super pissed off about it so he took his anger out on TLH.

Khan saw Shenx and was in such a hurry to just slaughter everyone, he just sliced off Shenx's legs assuming he'd already died /would die of blood loss.

Shenx did not die

So his whole mission is fueled by revenge to kill Khan, and consequently Pyrrhon's empire. He built his mech out of the remains of what was left in TLH and the dead clones that his parents killed. And went to the underground..

People generally respected this 12 year old boy walking in with no legs and a mech doing all sorts of crazy shit. He met lots of good friends (one in particular named Sho) and managed to scrape together enough money and people to revive TLH. It's an underground operation still, but they do operate.

Tl;dr shenx is mad at Khan for cutting his legs off, revives a rebellion group out of spite

Shenx actually discovered Marian and Khan again through Nea. I mean, Nea was Marian's daughter that night. so he wasn't very pleased to find that out.

He found out when he went to the hotel inn. They're all legally required to have a small pond for religious folk to meditate (treaty with nomads from a past war requires it). Shenx used it to take showers so when Nea showed up like to actually USE it to meditate he felt really bad. He tried to say "oh this is how I meditate uh uh uh" and she honestly couldn't give less of a shit. They started talking about religion and Nea just stared at him like, "you're reallty bad at this". Anyway the conversation lead to his missing legs, and he discovered taht she was Marian's daughter.

So she was almost stabbed that night if it weren't for Gamma. Nea agreed to bring Khan to TLH for Shenx in return for safety and a place to stay.

This is a storyline that probably wont be canon, but I still have it.

Khan is bound to a chair. Shenx just takes his unconscious body outside to the forest for a week. No one can find or contact him at all. No one knows where the hell he actually is or if he's ever coming back.

Shenx kind of went on this.. psychotic breakdown on Khan?

Khan is actually still alive, but he was BADLY injured. Shenx said he wanted to fight him to the death to show him what he had learned since the last time they met. Khan said "who the fuck are you? What's TLH?" So his lungs were pierced.

Shenx is a bartender so he's like the therapist for everyone. so combine that with his own problems and issues, all bottled up...

They fought so much that they died up in No Man's Land which is entirely out of Pyrrhon's control. Shenx almost pierced his heart, but purposefully missed. He just bascially tortured Khan out in the forest and left him there. Khan is still alive, but just barely. He is still up and kicking even post game.

Shenx just returns like nothing happened, but yk.. still shaken up about .. everything...

He crashed at Constantine's place first. He was covered in blood and crawling on the ground since his mech broke. He had the wide eyed bipolar schizophrenia look so Constantine surprisingly calmly took him in and treated him. If it weren't for Constantine, Shenx might've died. Or at least be chronically ill.

This storyline isn't canon, but it exists Uhhhhmmm

Personality

He is a laid-back, smart and sweet talking bartender. He flirts with his regulars and cooks up a really good drink. However, it’s all a facade. He acts as everyone’s therapist and has trouble putting himself first. He’s genuinely intelligent and kind-hearted towards those he likes. However, Shenx is not trusting of anyone at all. He tends to fly into a violent rage when he gets mad, and, when pushed, can grow psychotically strong. Shenx is a very sweet man. He flirts with … everyone really often, and enjoys watching them squirm. He’s very easygoing, but is a bit mischievous. He tends to be selfish and will do things that benefit him first. He does not hesitate to use blackmail or manipulate others to his needs
