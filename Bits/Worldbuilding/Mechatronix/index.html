Special | Shift

… and 1 Ultimate

A ‘special’ (or a ‘Shift’) is basically like a characters ‘secondary mode’. For example..

Gamma

uses her fists to fight using hand-to-hand combat. Using her ‘shift’ will make her stationary, but she will be able to used ranged attacks with her gun.

Constantine

normally uses an extremely large and heavy gun when he moves to attack people from a very long range. When he uses his ‘shift’, his attacks become less stationary but much weaker.

Honeycakes

normally uses a large obnoxious hammer which is also extremely slow. Surprisingly, she’s faster than Constantine. Using her ‘shift’ will change her attacks to be slower, but charged with magic and deal more damage and hits per second.

Nea

uses a sword to fight with people, but upon using her ‘shift’, she will shoot extra projectiles (sparks) with her sword, and a few of her attacks will be completely changed. (Ie her up attack will launch a projectile Instead of swinging upwards)

SHIFT

continued. There's a 3 second cooldown between each switch.

Also, when in shift state, you gain HEAT. When too much heat is gained, you begin to overheat and your accuracy is tanked and your speed decreases.

Maximum stack of heat before stunning is 10.

If you reach max heat, you snap out of it and are stunned for 5 seconds.

When playing the game, you’re given four total lives. You can distribute these Among all four of your teammates. (Ie 1 life for each, 2 lives for one and 2 lives for the other, 4 lives for one, etc).

You can ‘tag in’ your other teammates mid-game unless you are in a cooldown period, stunned, in the air, etc.

Abilities

Signature ability

what the character is known for; a passive ability. For example, Honeycakes takes no hitstuns to any attack that deals less than 10% of her max HP (extended to 20% while in SHIFT.

Extended Ability.

a “smart” reference to chord extensions. This is a characters secondary ability, which is less passive. This is activated in combat or by actions. For example, If Nea is hit mid-swing by a PHYSICAL attack, then trigger a parry slash. Also, gain the ability to cancel an enemy attack once every 5 seconds.

Death

Your save point.. is a fridge.

you can save an infinite amount of items inside of the fridge.

After you get a game over, you wake back up at the last fridge you saved at. You snap back out, like your death was nothing but a ‘bad dream’.

However, each item that you took with you remains at your death point. Your memory will linger in the place that you last died.

Failure to retrieve your ‘memory’ will result in all of your items and 70% of your cash being lost! (Cash of what you had on hand)

The World

The game is supposed to be this open world. and you’re supposed to be able to go through towns AND dungeons at the same time. Think of a metroidvania, but less ‘well done’.

This is because metroidvanias are non-linear and emphasize exploration through multiple routes. While CORE has a FEW of those elements, it is an ultimately linear story.

Gear

Equip-able items

  • CORE (permanent)

    • a permanent upgrade to your character. Dropped by major bosses and will upgrade ONE characters base stats

  • CORE (temporary)

    • `temporary buff. These are obtained by clones and minibosses, but will deteriorate over time. They can be repaired, but for a hefty price.

  • Fairy Bottle

  • a Small bottle filled with glitter. There is not a real fairy inside.

    • +5% max HP

  • Strawberry Quartz

  • A rare piece of quartz found in mines. Useless to most, but rumored to have magic enhancing properties.

    • Slightly increases magic damage

  • Amber

    • A small fossil attached to a chain. There is a star inside of it.

      • Slightly increases crit rate

  • Ribbon

    • A small and ragged red ribbon. Has two small pearls on each end.

      • Increases Stamina

  • Plastic Keychain

    • A translucent, pink tinted, plastic keychain in the shape of a pyramid. It is scratched from its many years of wear.

      • Increases maximum range

  • Headphones

    • Constantine’s favorite brand! Admittedly very good quality.

      • Increases walking speed

  • Delicate Flower

    • Found deep, deep in the mountains. Has crystalline, translucent leaves growing out of its center. Rumored to be an ancient medicinal herb.

      • If the user dies while having this item equipped, they are automatically revived with 10% health. It is destroyed right after.

  • Leather Pouch

    • Filled with various nuggets of something. The bag cannot be opened for some odd reason.

      • Slightly increases the chance of obtaining rare-er rewards.

  • Strange Doll

    • A doll made of string and wheat, with little black buttons for eyes. Emits a strange aura.

      • increases magic stamina usage, but greatly increases the strength of the magic.

  • TOKTOKTOKOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOK (goes off screen)

    • A transparent, dirty and cracked pair of two pink balls attached to a string. It’s a miracle the thing hasn’t broken yet.

      • Increases ATK

  • Old Console

    • Some old, unusable relic of the past. Stained with dirt, dust, and blood in every corner. The screen is cracked, and most of the buttons have fallen off, but it has too much nostalgia to give up.

      • Increases ADCH

  • Pink Crystal

  • String Bracelet

    • An old, blood-dusted, pathetic excuse for a bracelet. It’s black with small red and yellow beads at the end.

      • Decreases magic stamina usage.

  • Inkwell

Battle-usable items

  • Momma/Soy Beans

    • The character chews on it aggressively and spits it out at the enemy lmfao. Momma takes longer but deals more dmg.

  • Smoke bomb

    • Stuns lower-ranked enemies for easy pickings. Higher enemies will just miss more of their attacks. No effect on bosses.

  • Grenade

    • Self-explanatory. Takes up a good chunk of the screen and more powerful depending how close they are. Will damage the thrower.

  • Bottled poison

    • Self-explanatory. Will slow the enemy down.

  • Condensed poison

    • A stronger version that will damage the thrower.

  • Missile Launcher

    • Will explode upon meeting one obstacle, enemy or not.

  • HP/MP REGEN potion.

    • Thrown up like a splash potion and hits the user. Will heal them and any other enemy. Within its range.

  • Cleansing towel

    • Will remove all debuffs, but has a 1 second activation and cooldown.

  • Life Noodles

    • When thrown onto a friends dead body, it will revive them. You are vulnerable during this time.

  • Shield

    • Self-explanatory. Dies in 10 hits.

  • TOKTOTOOKTKTOKTOKOTKOTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOKTOK

    • Projectile. Will break and shatter and hit everything with small chip dmg. Will pierce shields.
